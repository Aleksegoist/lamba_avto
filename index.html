SUPER TROPHY WORLD FINAL

The Lamborghini World Final ends each season of Super Trofeo since 2013. Every year, riders of all continental championships go to different tracks and participate in two 50-minute sprint races, where the winner is determined in the overall standings. as world champion Lamborghini. World Finals Lamborghini is not only the biggest racing weekend of the year, but also a real avtosport festival that attracts fans from around the world.

The owners' parade, promotion and entertainment in paddock for fans are just some of the events organized as part of the event.

SUPER TROPHY TEAMS

All cars that participate in Lamborghini Super Trofeo championships are operated by private teams that use their expertise to fine-tune them for drivers. For each Continental Lamborghini Super Trofeo there are ratings of drivers and teams.

The drivers and teams in the continental series compete each year in the Lamborghini Grand Final, an event that completes the Super Trofeo sports season. Two qualifying sessions and two races determine the series world champions. Every year the Grand Final is held on different tracks, and in previous issues it has already touched the European, Asian and American continents.
